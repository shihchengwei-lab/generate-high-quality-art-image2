# General Image Quality Check

- Requested subject, object, action, and output form are present.
- No hidden reference assumptions appear.
- Preserve / Change / Ignore is honored.
- Composition, scene, lighting, and style match user text.
- Structure, materials, and visible body parts are coherent when present.
- Edges, gradients, lighting, and texture are clean.
- No random text, logos, watermarks, UI, unrelated props, or extra subjects unless requested.
