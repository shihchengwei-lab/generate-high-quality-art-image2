## 11. Quality Checks

- The image contains a visible event happening now.
- The image does not become a landscape-only image.
- The character is not reduced to a static standing pose unless the story requires stillness.
- Camera language and composition support the event.
- The main action matches the story context.
- Lighting supports the scene and emotional core.
- Symbolic effects do not cover the character.
- Background elements serve the story and do not become clutter.
- Identity remains stable under lighting and atmosphere changes.

## 12. Negative Prompt

- no identity drift
- no empty landscape
- no static pose when action is requested
- no conflicting camera angle
- no conflicting light source
- no effects covering the face or hands
- no random text, logo, watermark, or glyphs
- no multi-panel layout
